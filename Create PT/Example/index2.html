Custom Valentine Generator


This is a program that generates valentines for those you love!
Andrew always gets a valentine with 4 random adjectives, Jean always gets a valentine with 2 random adjectives, and Nick always gets a Valentine with 8 random adjectives. You can also generate your own custom Valentine to a friend of your choice. If you use 8 or more adjectives, your friend will be classified as your best friend, and one of their adjectives will be replaced with heart emojis!
